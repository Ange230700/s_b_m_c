This platform is a **modern all-in-one SaaS** built for couture ateliers.

The system unifies every core function of an atelier:

- client reception and measurement tracking,
- model selection and design validation,
- production planning and progress monitoring,
- appointment and delivery scheduling,
- team coordination and workload management,
- inventory, materials, and fabric tracking,
- revenue, expenses, and financial insights.

In addition to operations, it offers built-in tools for marketing and client engagement, including:

- automated follow-ups,
- reminders,
- customizable online showrooms for showcasing and selling creations.

The mission is simple: empower couture ateliers to serve their clients better, streamline operations, and grow their business with ease.
