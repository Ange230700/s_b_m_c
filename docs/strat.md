#### Workflow Efficiency

* 90% of production tasks tracked and updated in real-time
* 100% measurements entries are stored digitally
* Task assignment and progress tracking become daily habits for staff.

* Managers/owners check the dashboard daily
* Seamstresses complete their assigned tasks on time

* Measurement errors eliminated

#### Client Experience

* Clients receive automated reminders and updates without staff intervention
* Customer satisfaction (NPS) score of 9/10 or higher

* Onboarding time for new staff takes less than 1 day
* Front-desk staff can manage client intake and scheduling with minimal training

* Staff log in daily and consider the platform integral to their workflow
* No paper-based backups needed after 3 months of use

### Technical success

#### Performance & Reliability

* 99.9% uptime with minimal latency
* Seamless offline functionality for critical modules and without data loss

* Multi-tenant architecture supports 100+ ateliers and 1000+ clients per atelier without performance degradation

* Fully automated CI/CD pipeline for rapid releases and rollbacks
