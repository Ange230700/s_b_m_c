# Roles at Solange Bernard's

### **Receptionist/Manager**

- Greet clients and manage appointments
- Take and record detailed measurements
- Maintain client records and notes
- Coordinate with production team
- Handle client communications and follow-ups

### **Cutter**

- Interpret design sketches and patterns
- Cut fabric according to measurements and patterns
- Ensure precision and accuracy in cutting
- Collaborate with designers and seamstresses

### **Seamstress/Tailor**

- Sew garments according to design specifications
- Perform fittings and make necessary adjustments
- Ensure quality of stitching and finishing
- Collaborate with cutters and designers

### **Quality Control Specialist (Non-existant right now)**

- Inspect finished garments for accuracy and quality
- Compare garments against original measurements and designs
- Identify and flag any errors or deviations
- Document QC findings and communicate with production team
