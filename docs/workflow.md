# Workflow of Solange Bernard couture atelier

1. **Client Reception & Measurement**
   - Discuss client expectations and vision
   - Take detailed measurements

2. **Model Selection & Design Validation**
   - Client approves sketches or asks for revisions
   - Deliver appointment is scheduled

3. **Production Planning & Progress Monitoring**
   - Schedule production tasks in dual calendar system
   - Assign tasks to team members with deadlines
   - Monitor progress through production pipeline

4. **Appointment & Delivery Scheduling**
   - Schedule fitting appointments and delivery dates

5. **Production**

    - Cutting
    - Sewing
    - Quality Control (QC) checks

6. **Quality Control (currently missing)**

    - Does the final garment **match the approved design?**
    - Are measurements accurate?
    - Is stitching and finishing up to standard?
    - Are potential issues fixed before client appointment?
